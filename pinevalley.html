Pine Valley Golf Club

Overview:

Pine Valley Golf Club is renowned as one of the world's most exclusive and challenging golf courses. Located in Pine Valley, New Jersey, this private golf club has consistently been ranked at the top of various golf course lists and is a bucket-list destination for golf enthusiasts.

Course Information:

  • Location: Pine Valley, New Jersey, USA
  • Designer: George Arthur Crump and Harry S. Colt
  • Year Established: 1913
  • Par: 70
  • Yardage: Approximately 7,057 yards from the championship tees
  • Course Type: Private

Course Features:

  • Signature Hole: The 10th hole, known as "Hell's Half Acre," is one of the most famous and challenging par-5s in golf, featuring a sprawling waste area.
  • Unique Layout: Pine Valley is known for its rugged and natural layout, featuring sandy waste areas, towering pine trees, and undulating fairways.
  • Exclusive Membership: The club has a limited membership, and its exclusivity adds to its mystique.

Awards and Recognition:

Consistently ranked as the number one golf course in the world by various golf publications and experts. Hosted prestigious golf events, including the Walker Cup and George Arthur Crump Memorial Tournament.

Visitor Information:

  • Pine Valley Golf Club is a private club, and access is restricted to members and their guests.
  • Visitors are not allowed unless accompanied by a member.
